Lion facts

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey. Their coats are yellow-gold, and adult males have shaggy manes that range in color from blond to reddish-brown to black. The lion is very good at hunting. The lion sleeps during the day and hunts at night. The lion's long hair on his neck is called “mane” and it protects him. The lion is famous for its roar, large body and hunting ability.

Alligator facts

The American alligator is a large crocodilian with an armored body, short legs, a muscular tail and a long, rounded snout. This reptile nearly went extinct but is now considered a conservation success story.Alligators are large reptiles with short legs, long tails, tough skin, sharp teeth and wide snouts. As reptiles, they are cold-blooded animals that lay eggs and are covered in hard plates.
